The Decarb Energy Advantage

Decarb Energy turns PFAS-contaminated wastewater sludge into American-made, sustainable biofuel while destroying the PFAS “forever chemicals” in the waste—on-site, with zero partner capex, using modular equipment.

Zero Partner Capex

Infrastructure-as-a-Service means Decarb finances, owns, and operates the Biosolids+™ Trailers—so you get on-site PFAS destruction and sludge-to-biofuel capability without a capital project.

  • No partner debt, procurement maze, or EPC risk
  • Simple per-ton service pricing under a long-term agreement
  • Profit share of biofuel and credits cash flow with host facilities—aligning incentives from day one

Lower Sludge Costs

We structure per-ton processing to beat the haul-and-dispose status quo—incineration, landfill, or land application—so savings can start immediately. Predictable per-ton processing reduces go-forward volatility and mitigates stranded sludge risk as disposal options tighten.

  • Long-term price locks bring stable $/ton economics and budget certainty
  • Lower OPEX without municipal CAPEX
  • We win only when taxpayers save money
  • Less exposure to disposal capacity constraints

Destroy PFAS On-Site

PFAS is the design case—not an afterthought. Our PFAS Lock Box targets a 99.999% destruction design goal, with measurement and reporting to document liability reduction.

  • Destroy PFAS on-site instead of shipping the problem somewhere else
  • Keep PFAS out of drinking water, waterways, and soils
  • Cleaner air, fewer trucks, stronger local resilience
  • Reduce long-term PFAS liability and uncertainty
  • Pair on-site processing with cleaner-burning biofuel to deliver visible community benefits

Bolt-On, Modular, & Factory-Built

No permanent infrastructure. Trailers roll in, sit on-site, and bolt onto your existing dewatering line—starting where your current process ends. Factory-built, standardized modules avoid multi-year construction schedules and keep costs predictable—without the change-order spiral of stick-build projects.

  • Permit-light posture with minimal siteworks
  • Standard interfaces for faster tie-in and commissioning
  • Repeatable modules built and tested off-site
  • Streamlined permitting and equipment siting

Turnkey Deployments & Scalable Technology

You run a water utility—not a PFAS destruction specialist or biofuels developer—so we don’t ask you to become one. Decarb Energy fabricates, finances, owns, operates, services, and maintains the equipment. You provide site access, a parking area, and data. Residual solids remain under existing disposal contracts.

  • Operator-light deployment with remote monitoring
  • Decarb handles O&M and performance responsibility
  • Need more throughput? Scale incrementally with LNG-style trains

No Grid, Utilities, or On-Site Emissions

Designed for islanded operation with no utility ties and no interconnection queues—so deployments can move quickly, even at constrained sites.

  • No grid interconnection costs or delays
  • Deploy in days, not years (site dependent)
  • No on-site stack emissions keep local air clean, simplify air permits, and accelerate deployment

Reliable & Proven Tech

Standardized modules are based on industry-standard biosolids dewatering trailers with conventional tie-ins—built for operator confidence and remote monitoring. To do so, we've integrated industrially-proven technology that has existed for 100+ years—no untested science experiments.

  • Proven components with familiar interfaces for your operations team
  • Engineered for wastewater uptime and maintainability
  • Built for wastewater operators who need budget certainty, compliance confidence, and minimal site disruption

Why can’t clean energy development be as simple as setting up a rental power generator? No smokestacks, grid connects, permanent infrastructure, or science experiments. Just a system of simple, permit-light trailers that roll up, sit on site, and bolt on to existing infrastructure.
