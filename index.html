About Us:

As an independent project developer, we partner directly with landfills, municipalities, and stakeholders to decarbonize operations, efficiently produce renewable biofuel, and transform biogas liabilities into stable revenue streams that support local investment and economic vitality.

The Decarb Energy platform deploys our proprietary technology and innovative biogas conversion system, which efficiently transforms stranded biogas into clean fuels while simultaneously decarbonizing the landfill. Our Technology:

Decarb Energy has developed proprietary technology to capture stranded landfill emissions and efficiently convert the waste gases into high-quality sustainable biofuels.

Across the United States today, landfills of all sizes produce biogas, which is a natural byproduct as waste decomposes underground. This gas stream is a mixture of methane (the main component of natural gas), biogenic carbon dioxide, and small volumes of trace impurities. Biogas that escapes into the atmosphere is detrimental to local air quality and contributes to climate change—Over a 20-year period, methane traps 84 times more heat than carbon dioxide, making it a potent driver of climate change.

Often dismissed as waste and an environmental liability, landfill biogas can be a valuable energy resource when harnessed in partnership with the right developer. Projects that capture landfill biogas and transform it into renewable biofuels ensure that the methane and carbon dioxide don't escape into the atmosphere. This not only improves local air quality and mitigates climate change, but it also creates another source of valuable fuel that we can use to power our everyday lives.

Development Benefits:

Our projects and the renewable biofuels we produce are designed to drive a meaningful positive environmental impact, catalyze energy security & affordability, and maximize the overall benefits that we create for our project partners, stakeholders, and local communities. These benefits include:

  • Reduced Emissions & Pollution: We reduce GHG emissions and local pollution in our two core activities. First, we capture biogas before is escapes into the atmosphere. Second, the renewable biofuels we produce burn cleaner and are safer to handle than gasoline and diesel. Our end-to-end decarbonization solution ensures that landfill emissions are captured, converted, and utilized—turning waste into fuel. Decarb Energy's proprietary technology and unique business model also empower our landfill partners to eliminate most scope 1 & 2 emissions, from the landfill vent all the way to the fuel pump.
  • Energy Security & American Dynamism: Our operations are independent from the volatile petrochemical markets, meaning that our locally-produced fuels are insulated from price shocks and not reliant on complex supply chains. This strengthens our community with diverse energy sources, promoting cost-effective energy resiliency, which protects consumers, combats inflation before it happens, and decreases our dependence on foreign energy.
  • Improved Local Air Quality: Our projects virtually eliminate the release of harmful pollutants into the air we breathe, including sulfur oxides (SOX), nitrogen oxides (NOX), ozone- and smog-forming compounds (VOCs), and soot (particulates, or PMX).
  • Economic Development & Job Creation: Decarb Energy fosters economic growth by: (1) Creating high-paying, next-generation energy jobs, especially in rural communities; (2) Building meaningful partnerships with landfills, local governments, industry, and stakeholders; and (3) Delivering long-term value to our partners and the communities we serve.

Together with our partners, we are paving the way for a cleaner, more sustainable, and energy-abundant future.
