The Decarb Energy Advantage

Decarb Energy turns PFAS-contaminated wastewater sludge into American-made, sustainable biofuel while destroying the PFAS “forever chemicals” in the waste—on-site, with zero partner capex, using modular equipment.

Zero Partner Capex

Infrastructure-as-a-Service means Decarb finances, operates, and maintains our Biosolids+™ Trailers—so you get on-site PFAS destruction and sludge-to-biofuel capability without a capital project.

  • No capital budgets, partner debt, procurement mazes, or construction risks
  • Simple per-ton service pricing structured to save utilities money—aligning incentives from day one
  • Upside: Profit share of biofuel + credits cash flow with partnering facilities

Lower Sludge Costs

We structure per-ton processing to beat the haul-and-dispose status quo—incineration, landfill, or land application—so savings can start immediately. Predictable per-ton processing reduces go-forward volatility and mitigates stranded sludge risk as disposal options tighten.

  • Long-term price locks bring stable $/ton economics and budget certainty
  • Lower OPEX without municipal CAPEX
  • We win only when taxpayers save money
  • Reduce exposure to tightening sludge disposal capacity & regulations

Destroy PFAS On-Site

PFAS is the design case—not an afterthought. Our PFAS Lock Box targets a 99.999% destruction design goal, with measurement and reporting to document liability reduction.

  • Destroy PFAS on-site instead of shipping the problem somewhere else
  • Keep PFAS out of drinking water, waterways, and soils
  • Cleaner air, fewer trucks, stronger local resilience
  • Reduce long-term PFAS liability and uncertainty
  • Pair on-site processing with cleaner-burning biofuel to deliver visible community benefits

Bolt-On, Modular, & Factory-Built

No permanent infrastructure. Trailers roll in, sit on-site, and bolt onto your existing dewatering line—starting where your current process ends. Factory-built, standardized modules avoid multi-year construction schedules and keep costs predictable—without the change-order spiral of stick-build projects.

  • Permit-light equipment with no permanent on-site infrastructure
  • Standard interfaces for faster tie-in + commissioning
  • Repeatable modules fabricated & tested off-site
  • Streamlined permits & equipment siting

Turnkey Deployments & Scalable Technology

You run a water utility—not a PFAS destruction specialist or biofuels developer—so we don’t ask you to become one. Decarb Energy fabricates, finances, operates, services, & maintains our equipment. You provide site access, a parking area, & data. Residual solids remain under existing disposal contracts.

  • Decarb finances, operates, & maintains our operator-light equipment
  • Decarb handles all O&M &, performance, & expenses
  • Need more throughput? Scale incrementally with LNG-style trains

No Grid, Utilities, or On-Site Emissions

To be truly turnkey, the Biosolids+ Trailer system is designed for islanded operations with no grid connection, utility ties, or long interconnection queues—so deployments can move quickly, even at constrained sites. Our equipment rolls up, bolts on, and starts converting waste into biofuel.

  • No grid interconnection equipment, costs, or delays
  • Deploy modular trailers in days, not in years for bespoke projects
  • No on-site stack emissions keep local air clean, simplify air permits, and accelerate timelines + community benefits

Reliable & Proven Tech

Our standardized modules are based on industry-standard 'biosolids dewatering trailers' with conventional tie-ins—built for operator confidence and remote monitoring. To do so, we've integrated industrially-proven technology that has existed for 100+ years—no untested science experiments.

  • Proven components with familiar interfaces for your operations team
  • Engineered for wastewater: Reliable, rugged, and maintainable
  • Built for wastewater operators who need budget certainty, compliance confidence, and minimal site disruption

Meet the Biosolids+™ Trailer

Why can’t clean energy development be as simple as setting up a rental power generator? No smokestacks, grid connects, on-site permanent infrastructure, or science experiments. Just a system of simple, permit-light trailers that roll up, sit on site, and bolt on to existing infrastructure.
