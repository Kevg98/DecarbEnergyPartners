About Us

Decarb Energy Partners drives decarbonization and energy security by transforming organic waste into clean, American-made biofuels. Our patent-pending equipment turns even the toughest waste stream into a perpetual renewable natural gas well that produces a sustainable alternative for diesel and gasoline.

As an independent project developer, we partner directly with wastewater treatment plants, farmers, landfills, municipalities, and their stakeholders to decarbonize their operations, produce renewable biofuel, and transform waste liabilities into energy developments that drive local investment and economic vitality.

We turn organic waste streams into perpetual natural gas wells and clean American-made biofuels.
