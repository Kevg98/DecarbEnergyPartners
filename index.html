Decarbonizing Landfills with Innovative Biofuels Production

About Us

Decarb Energy Partners is strengthening American industry, decarbonization, and energy independence by transforming harmful landfill emissions into clean, sustainable renewable bio-fuels.

Our platform deploys efficient and innovative new technology that decarbonizes landfills and transforms stranded biogas into clean renewable fuels. By capturing and utilizing landfill biogas, we reduce greenhouse gas emissions and other harmful pollutants while supporting the transition to cleaner energy sources. We prioritize keeping our biofuels close to where we produce them, which ensures that local communities benefit the most from our clean, locally-produced renewable biofuels.

Our Technology

Decarb Energy has designed and developed proprietary technology and processes to efficiently capture landfill emissions and efficiently convert it into high-quality sustainable biofuels.

Across the United States today, landfills of all sized produce biogas, which is a natural byproduct as waste decomposes underground. This gas stream is a mixture of methane (natural gas), biogenic carbon dioxide, and small volumes of trace impurities. Biogas that excapes into the atmosphere is detrimental to local air quality and contributed to climate change--Over a 20-year horizon, methane is 84x worse than carbon dioxide by trapping more heat in our atmosphere.

Capturing this landfill biogas and turning it into biofuels ensures that it never escapes into the atmosphere. This not only improves local air quality and mitigates climate changes, but it also creates another source of valuable fuel that we can use to power our everyday lives.

Environmental Impact

By capturing landfill emissions and other greenhouse gases before they escape into the atmosphere, Decarb Energy’s platform plays a critical role in mitigating climate change, improving local air quality, and producing economic clean biofuels.

Our projects and the renewable biofuels they produce are designed to maximize our environmental impact and the benefits we bring to our project partners, stakeholders, and local communities :

  • Lower EmissionsDecarb Energy reduces emissions from landfills and produces renewable biofuels that are safer and burn cleaner than fossil-derived gasoline and diesel. We provide our landfill partners with a solution for end-to-end decarbonization, from the landfill vent to the fuel pump
  • Energy Security & American Dynamism: Additional locally-produced fuels independent of volatile petrochemical markets and global supply chains. This protects consumers and decreases our dependence on foreign energy
  • Improved Local Air Quality: Our projects nearly eliminates harful pollutants, including sulfur oxides (SOx), nitrogen oxides (SOx), ozone- and smog-forming compounds (VOCs), and soot (particulates, or PMx)
  • Economic Development & Job Creation: Decarb Energy's projects create high-paying nex-gen energy jobs, and we are committed to maximizing value for our project partners, stakeholders, and the communities in which we operate

Together with our partners, we are paving the way for a cleaner, sustainable, and secure energy future.
