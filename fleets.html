Decarbonize & Future-Proof Your Fleet

Preparing your fleet to reach net-zero can be as simple as switching to a sustainable fuel source. Decarb Energy's proprietary biofuels are drop-in ready, burn more cleanly, and carbon-negative. This means more carbon and pollution are absorbed and destroyed in making the fuels than are released when they are used.

  • Drop-in Ready: No expensive retrofits
  • Sustainable: Cleaner and carbon-negative
  • Energy Security: American-made and renewable

Decarb ZeroMax™

For heavy-duty applications, Decarb ZeroMax™ is a no-compromise, high-cetane diesel alternative that delivers exceptional performance without sacrificing sustainability.

  • High Performance: 50+ cetane rating
  • Maximum Safety: No carcinogenic diesel fumes
  • No Compromises: Cheaper & greener than EVs

Decarb Zero102™

Formulated as a high-octane (102), high-performance gasoline alternative, Decarb Zero102™ offers a seamless transition to sustainable fueling with no loss in power.

  • High Performance: 102-octane rating
  • Unparalleled Safety: Safer than gasoline
  • Versatile: Multiple blends available

Decarb Zero102™ is the first step in preparing your fleet for the net-zero economy.
